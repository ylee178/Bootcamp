Hey! I'm a Sliding Footer

What's a Sliding Footer? Well I'm a cool little element which can be hidden from view, and revealed when the user wants to see me.

What can you use me for? Well look at all this stuff:

  • Sales information
  • Important updates
  • Unobtrusive about panel
  • Or just a good ol' footer

There are obviously many other uses, but these are the few useful ones I can think of.
